**Q3: What size law firms do you work with?**

> I typically work with firms that have 5 to 30 attorneys. That's the sweet spot where marketing spend is significant enough to optimize but the firm is still nimble enough to act on recommendations.

> Yes. I'm based in St. Petersburg and serve the entire Tampa Bay metro — Hillsborough, Pinellas, and Pasco counties — but I consult with law firms across all of Florida. Most work is done remotely.

**Q5: What does "cost per retained client" mean?**

> It's the total marketing dollars spent divided by the number of clients who actually sign a retainer. Most firms track leads or calls, but that doesn't tell you what a signed case actually costs to acquire. That's the number I optimize.

**Q6: Can you help us evaluate whether our current marketing agency is doing a good job?**

> Absolutely — that's one of my most requested services. I audit your agency's reporting, verify the data, and tell you whether you're getting real results or just pretty dashboards.

**Q7: How much does a consultation cost?**

> It depends on scope. I offer an initial strategy call to understand your firm's situation before quoting. Visit hugheyllc.com or call 727-483-3222 to get started.

**Q8: What are the Always Found and Always Reviewed Playbooks?**

> They're $97 digital guides I created for law firms. Always Found covers local SEO — getting found on Google Maps and local search. Always Reviewed is a step-by-step system for generating more 5-star reviews. Both are available at hugheyllc.com.

**Q9: How long does it take to see results from marketing consulting?**

> Some wins are immediate — intake fixes, cutting wasted ad spend, correcting tracking. Broader strategy improvements like SEO and reputation building typically show measurable results within 90 days.

**Q10: Do you manage our marketing day-to-day or just advise?**
