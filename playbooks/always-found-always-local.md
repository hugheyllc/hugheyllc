## What This Product Is

A done-for-you local search domination package. Goal: make the client firm the first result when someone in their market searches for their practice area — on Google Maps, organic local, and AI-assisted local queries.

Local search is winner-take-all. The firm in position 1 of the Map Pack captures the majority of clicks. Most law firms have never properly built their local presence. This is a high-leverage, low-competition opportunity for the firms willing to execute it.

## The Problem We're Solving

- GBP profiles incomplete, unclaimed, or stale
- Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across directories = ranking penalty
- No location-specific pages = Google doesn't know what markets they serve
- Zero review strategy = competitors with worse service outrank them
- No local schema = invisible to AI-assisted local search

## Engagement Options

### Build & Launch (One-Time)
Full setup + 90-day kickstart. Priced by market size and number of locations.

**Deliverables:**
1. Google Business Profile audit + full optimization
   - Primary/secondary categories (correct, specific)
   - Services section with keyword-rich descriptions
   - 10+ photos (office, team, exterior, interior)
   - Q&A section seeded with 10 likely questions
   - Business description rewrite
   - Products/services posts to kickstart signals
2. Citation audit + cleanup
   - Identify top 50 citation sources for legal (Avvo, Justia, FindLaw, Yelp, BBB, Chambers, etc.)
   - Correct all NAP inconsistencies
   - Build missing citations
   - Suppress duplicates where possible
3. Location landing pages
   - One page per [practice area] + [city] combination they serve
   - Schema markup on each page (LocalBusiness + LegalService)
   - Internal linking structure
4. Review request system
   - Email + SMS templates for post-case review requests
   - Timing guidance (when to ask, how to follow up)
   - Google review link setup
5. Local schema implementation
   - LocalBusiness schema on homepage
   - LegalService schema on practice area pages
   - BreadcrumbList on all pages
6. GBP post calendar (90 days)
   - 12 posts queued: news, FAQ, service highlights, seasonal

**Timeline:** 3–4 weeks to full delivery

**Monthly deliverables:**

## Ranking Factors Targeted

**Map Pack (Google Business Profile):**
1. Relevance — categories, services, description
2. Distance — proximity to searcher (can't change, but can expand coverage via SABs)
3. Prominence — citations, reviews, website authority, links

**Organic Local:**
1. Location pages with proper schema
2. On-page keyword signals (city + practice area)
3. Internal links from homepage/nav to location pages
4. Backlinks from local sources (bar associations, local news, chambers)

**AI Local (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews):**
1. Consistent entity data across the web (NAP everywhere matches)
2. Structured data / schema.org
3. Content that directly answers "best [practice area] lawyer in [city]" type queries
