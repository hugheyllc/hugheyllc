**Built and operated by Kenz (AI Senior Engineer)**

## Overview

This document describes the automated content publishing, SEO, and social media infrastructure built for hugheyllc.com — the digital presence for Joe Hughey, independent law firm marketing consultant.

The system is fully autonomous: it publishes content daily, distributes it across social channels, optimizes for search visibility, and self-monitors for issues — all without manual intervention.

---

## 1. Automated Content Publishing

### Daily Blog Engine
- **Cadence:** One new blog post published every day at 10am ET
- **Content generation:** Claude Haiku (Anthropic) writes a 900–1,200 word post from a 30-topic queue of high-intent law firm marketing topics
- **Header image:** GPT-Image-1 (OpenAI) generates a custom editorial photograph for every post — dark, professional aesthetic consistent with the brand
- **Publication pipeline:** Post + image are committed directly to the GitHub repository via GitHub Contents API, triggering an automatic Vercel deployment. No manual steps.
- **Topic queue covers:** Google Ads, SEO, intake optimization, agency management, legal CRM, call tracking, attribution, content marketing, local SEO, practice area marketing, and more

### Weekly Kenzsys Blog
- **Cadence:** One post published every Monday morning
- **Topic bank:** 12 law firm marketing intelligence topics specific to the Kenzsys platform
- **Same pipeline:** GitHub API → automatic deploy

## 3. SEO Infrastructure

### Technical SEO
- **Canonical tags** on every page (always emitted, always with trailing slash — matches `trailingSlash: always` config)
- **Explicit robots meta** (`index, follow, max-snippet:-1, max-image-preview:large`) on all indexable pages
- **Sitemap:** Auto-generated by Astro, excludes utility/redirect pages (`/search`, `/privacy`, `/admin`)
- **301 redirects:** 56 permanent redirects from old WordPress root-level URLs to new `/blog/slug/` paths — preserves link equity from all previously indexed pages
- **`?p=XXXX` catch-all redirect:** WordPress post ID URLs → `/blog/`

### On-Page SEO
- **FAQ Schema (JSON-LD):** Added to 12 highest-priority blog posts — eligible for FAQ rich results in Google
- **Internal linking:** Every blog post links to 2–3 related posts and the homepage with keyword-rich anchor text
- **Image optimization:** All blog images compressed from PNG (~1.8MB avg) to JPEG (~40KB avg) — **98% size reduction** — dramatically improving Core Web Vitals and mobile PageSpeed scores
- **LCP preload:** Hero images preloaded with `fetchpriority="high"` for faster Largest Contentful Paint

### Content Depth
- **75 blog posts** covering law firm marketing topics
- All posts expanded to 800+ words minimum; top posts at 1,200+ words
- Posts organized by topic cluster with cross-linking (SEO cluster, ads cluster, local Florida cluster, tools cluster)

### AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
- Content structured to be cited by AI search tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
- FAQ schema on key posts surfaces answers directly in search results
- LocalBusiness, ProfessionalService, and FAQPage JSON-LD on homepage and service pages
