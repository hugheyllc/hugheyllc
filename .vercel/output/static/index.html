The Real Problem

Most Law Firms Are Measuring the Wrong Thing

Vanity Metrics

Marketing teams celebrate impressions, clicks, and leads. But nobody asks: how many of those leads actually become retained clients?

Siloed Data

Your website, Google Ads, CRM, intake forms, and billing system don't talk to each other. Marketing doesn't know what closes cases.

Budget Waste

You're spending on channels that don't convert while underfunding the ones that do. No system to connect spend to retained clients.

Lead Leakage

53% of qualified leads never reach a consultation. Form submission to first call takes days. Intake process loses them to competitors.

No Strategy

You react to vendor pitches instead of following a plan. "Let's try SEO" one quarter, "now PPC" the next. Inconsistent results.

Wrong KPIs

You can't answer: "What does it cost us to acquire a retained client?" So you can't make rational budget decisions.
